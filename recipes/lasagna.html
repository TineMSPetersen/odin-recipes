Homemade lasagna

This is a wonderful lasagna recipe made in the way your grandmother surely crafted them.

  • Meat
  • Milk
  • Tomatoes
  • cheese
  • Pasta plates
  1. Brown the meat in a big pot
  2. Add milk and tomato to make a lasagna sauce
  3. Put together the lasagna with the sauce and pasta plates
  4. Sprinkle on cheese and bake for 20 minutes at 200 C
